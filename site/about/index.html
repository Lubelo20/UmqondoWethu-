Our Story

We deliver on time,
nomakanjani.

Umqondo Wethu Car Movers (UWCM) is a vehicle and truck transporting company based in Durban. Since the company was founded, it has been providing vehicle moving services for both new and used vehicles and trucks to dealerships, fleet companies, and private customers nationwide.

The company started with supplying transportation services to one dealership — McCarthy Nissan Gateway — and has since grown to serve more than 200 dealerships nationwide.

UWCM has also extended its service scope from transporting vehicles to providing driver supply to service departments, including chauffeurs, smash-and-grab installations, windscreen stone chip repairs, and more.
